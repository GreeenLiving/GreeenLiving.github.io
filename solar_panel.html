Overview

Harnessing the sun's abundant energy to generate electricity, solar panels have long been the cornerstone of residential renewable energy solutions. Panels are directly installed on the roofs of houses and use photovoltaic systems to convert sunlight into electricity. With advancements in technology and tax rebates, the installation and maintenance of solar panels is becoming more accessible and affordable. Research has shown that homeowners can save up to $1,500 yearly by switching to solar panels.

Costs

Installation costs for solar panels range between $10,000 and $25,000 based on the size of the house and number of panels required. However, location is a significant factor that can affect your costs: some regions receive more sunlight than others. Additionally, household energy usage can also affect the number of solar panels needed: a larger house and family usually correlates with higher energy consumption.
